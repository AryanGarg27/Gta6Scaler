## When Do GTA 6 Pre-Orders Open?

Rockstar confirmed via their official Newswire on June 18, 2026 that pre-orders for Grand Theft Auto VI begin on **June 25, 2026** across digital storefronts and select physical retailers.

There is a reason this announcement carries more weight than just hype: PlayStation Store and Xbox Store policy does not allow games to open for pre-order until they are within 12 months of launch. By opening them now, Rockstar has effectively locked November 19 in place. A further delay to 2027 is off the table.

The same announcement revealed the official cover art for the first time — protagonists **Lucia Caminos and Jason Duval** against a neon-soaked Vice City backdrop — and confirmed that exact pricing and edition details drop alongside pre-orders on June 25.

## GTA 6 Price: What to Expect

Official pricing drops June 25. Here is what retailer leaks and industry analysis currently point to:

### Standard Edition — ~$79.99 / ~₹7,499

The base game on PS5 or Xbox Series X/S. Full single-player campaign with Lucia and Jason across Leonida, plus access to GTA 6 Online. This is the edition most players will buy.

### Deluxe Edition — ~$99.99 / ~₹9,499

Expected to add a digital bonus pack on top of the base game. Based on Rockstar's pattern with GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2, this tier typically includes GTA Online currency, an exclusive vehicle, and cosmetic items for the protagonists.

### Collector's Edition — ~$200+ / ~₹18,999+

French retailer FNAC leaked five PS5 SKUs ahead of the announcement, with the highest tier at €199.99. If GTA 6 follows the RDR2 Collector's Box model, expect a steelbook case, physical art book, map poster, and in-game bonuses in premium packaging.

> **On the $200 base price rumor:** Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick addressed this directly, stating the company "won't be greedy" on price. The $200 figure refers to Collector's Edition territory only — not the standard game.

## GTA 6 Pre-Order Bonuses: What to Expect

Rockstar has not confirmed pre-order bonuses yet — those details arrive June 25. Based on every major Rockstar launch, here is what is widely anticipated:

- **GTA Online currency** — a GTA$ credit drop (previous standard packs started at $500,000 GTA$)
- **Exclusive vehicle** — a limited car, motorcycle, or watercraft not available to non-pre-order buyers at launch
- **Character cosmetics** — outfit packs, weapon wraps, or tattoos for Lucia and/or Jason
- **Retailer-exclusive bonuses** — GameStop and Best Buy have historically offered unique skin packs

**Important note:** Pre-order bonuses in GTA titles have never been permanently exclusive. Rockstar typically adds these items to the in-game store within a few months. If you are undecided, there is no hard FOMO argument.

## Everything Confirmed About GTA 6

**Release:** November 19, 2026 on PS5, PS5 Pro, Xbox Series X, and Xbox Series S.

**PC:** No launch date confirmed. Based on the GTA V timeline (PC arrived 18 months after console), expect a PC release in 2027–2028 at earliest.
