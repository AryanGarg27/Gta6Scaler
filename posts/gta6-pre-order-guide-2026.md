## GTA 6 Editions: What's Confirmed

Rockstar has announced exactly two editions. That is it — no third tier, no steelbook, no collector's bundle.

### Standard Edition — $79.99 / ~₹6,999

The base game on PS5 or Xbox Series X/S. Full single-player campaign with Lucia and Jason across Leonida, plus access to GTA 6 Online. Available digitally and as a physical box (see the no-disc note below).

### Ultimate Edition — $99.99 / ~₹8,499

The premium digital-only tier. Rockstar has not yet detailed exactly what the Ultimate Edition includes beyond the base game, but based on their history with GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2, expect a GTA Online currency pack, an exclusive vehicle, and character cosmetic items for Lucia and Jason. Full contents are expected to be listed on the storefront.

> **Note:** The Ultimate Edition is **digital only**. There is no physical version of the Ultimate Edition.

## The No-Disc Situation: What You Need to Know

This is the detail most people are missing. Rockstar confirmed in their June 24 Newswire that **physical copies of GTA 6 Standard Edition do not include a disc**. The box contains only a download code.

This means:
- You still need a working internet connection to install the game even from a "physical" copy
- The game cannot be resold or lent to a friend in the traditional sense
- PS5 Digital Edition and disc-based PS5 owners are in the same position
- The disc slot on your console is irrelevant for GTA 6

If you were planning to buy a physical copy specifically to avoid a large download, that option does not exist. The physical box is essentially boxed packaging for a digital license.

The only advantage of buying physical over digital is payment timing — more on that below.

## How to Pre-Order GTA 6 Right Now

### On PS5 (PlayStation Store)

1. Open PlayStation Store on your PS5, or go to **store.playstation.com**
2. Search "Grand Theft Auto VI"
3. Select Standard or Ultimate Edition
4. Complete payment — **PlayStation charges you immediately**
5. Game pre-loads closer to November 19 launch

### On Xbox Series X/S (Microsoft Store)

1. Open Microsoft Store on your Xbox or go to **xbox.com/store**
2. Search "Grand Theft Auto VI"
3. Select your edition and complete the order
4. **Xbox only charges up to 10 days before the game releases** — your money stays in your account until November

### Physical Copy (Standard only — no disc)

Available through Amazon India, Flipkart, and other retailers. Physical copies are download codes in a box. The key payment advantage: Amazon does not charge until the item ships, meaning your money is not taken until just before launch day.

## GTA 6 Pre-Order FAQ

Rockstar confirmed that physical Standard Edition copies contain only a download code. No reason was given. It follows a growing trend in the games industry of disc-free physical releases.

**Is the Ultimate Edition available physically?**
No. The Ultimate Edition is digital only.

**Will a Collector's Edition be announced later?**
Rockstar has not announced one and has made no indication one is coming. Do not wait for a collector's edition that may never be announced.

**Can I play GTA 6 on PC?**
Not in 2026. No PC release date has been confirmed.

**Is GTA 6 available on PS4 or Xbox One?**
No. PS5 and Xbox Series X/S only.

**How large is the download?**
No official file size confirmed. Clear 150–250GB on your console before launch day.

**Is GTA 6 Online free with the game?**
Expected yes — included with every copy of the game, following the GTA V model.

**When exactly can I start playing?**
Pre-loading begins on November 19 — the launch day itself, not earlier. You cannot pre-load the game in advance.
